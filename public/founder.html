Meet the Founder

Cherono Ochieng'

Cherono Ochieng' is the visionary behind Code Craft Haven. The idea for Code Craft Haven was born during a time when I was seeking to connect with like-minded visionaries in the Nakuru Tech Space, but that quest didn’t lead to much success. This made me realize that if such a community didn’t exist, I would create one. My goal was to provide mentorship and share the little knowledge I had in programming.

After mentoring a few passionate individuals, I felt the drive to participate in a hackathon. However, my search for one close by led me only to events in Nairobi. It was then that I realized the universe was pushing me toward a path that truly resonated with my career and passion. This experience highlighted a gap — Nakuru lacked such tech events, especially hackathons. Thus, I decided to build a platform that would bring tech enthusiasts together right here, in Nakuru.

I have a gut feeling that this is the right direction. We will eventually bring and build tech companies that will elevate the Nakuru tech space to a new level, transforming it into a thriving hub of innovation and opportunity.

"At Code Craft Haven, we turn visionary dreams into reality, fostering innovation and growth where it's needed most." - Cherono Ochieng'
